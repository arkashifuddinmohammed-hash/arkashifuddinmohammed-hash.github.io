This project carries 4 image plates for now. The first plate holds the major image, and the following plates act as replaceable placeholders for final drawings, renders, site photos, process frames, or material studies.

Reading

What this page is trying to hold.

The work is presented as a time-based spatial emotion, not a static image set. Floating City keeps its focus on through atmosphere and scale, while the composition leaves room for drawings, site notes, and expanded documentation.
