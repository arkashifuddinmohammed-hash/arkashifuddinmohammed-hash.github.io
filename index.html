About

Designing through atmosphere, systems, memory, and narrative.

Kashif Mohammed is an architect from NIT Calicut exploring architecture through cinematic space, emotional atmosphere, conceptual systems, and layered storytelling.

His work investigates the relationship between architecture and perception — where spaces are experienced not only physically, but emotionally and psychologically.
